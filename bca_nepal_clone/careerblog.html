BCA vs CSIT in Nepal: Which One Should You Choose in 2026?

As we approach 2026, the demand for skilled IT professionals in Nepal is at an all-time high. However, for a student graduating high school, the choice between Bachelor in Computer Applications (BCA) and B.Sc. Computer Science and Information Technology (CSIT) remains the most daunting hurdle.

While both paths lead to a successful career in tech, their methodologies, academic rigor, and professional outcomes differ significantly. This guide breaks down exactly which degree fits your specific goals.

Feature BCA B.Sc.CSIT
Focus Application & Software Dev Theory & Computation Science
Eligibility Any Stream (D+ average) Science Stream (Physics/Math)
Math Rigor Moderate High (Advanced Calculus)
