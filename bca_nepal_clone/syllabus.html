BCA Syllabus Overview

The Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA) syllabus in Nepal is designed to produce high-quality computer application developers and IT professionals. While the core foundation remains consistent across all universities (TU, PU, KU), specific credit hours and elective offerings may vary.

The curriculum is unique blend of Computer Science, Management, and Liberal Arts. Students begin with fundamental logic and programming in the first year, move into core systems and databases in the second, and specialize in high-demand techologies like Data Science, Machine Learning, and Enterprise Jave in their final years. Every BCA program culminates in a comprehensive Final Year Project and internship, bridging the gap between academic theory and industry requirements.
