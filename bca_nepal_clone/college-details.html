About the College

Kathmandu Model College (KMC), established in 2003, has consistently ranked among the top private colleges in Nepal. Our vision is to empower students with holistic education that blends academic rigor with practical industry insights.Located in the serene yet accessible hub of Balkumari, KMC provides a state-of-the-art learning environment. Over the last two decades, we have fostered a culture of innovation, specifically within the Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA) program, producing tech leaders who are now driving Nepal's digital economy.

Admission Process

1

Entrance Exam (CMAT)

Candidates must appear for the Central Management Admission Test (CMAT) conducted by TU.

2

College Interview

Shortlisted candidates will be invited for a personal interview at the KMC campus to assess their technical aptitude.

3

Enrollment & Orientation

Successful candidates can secure their seat by fulfilling the document requirements and attending the bridge course orientation.
